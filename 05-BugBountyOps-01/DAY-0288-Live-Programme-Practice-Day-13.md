# Day 288 — Live Programme Practice Day 13: Community Reading and Technique Update

> "Researchers who stop learning stop earning. The threat landscape moves faster
> than any static skill set. Today you step back from the target and invest
> in the techniques that will improve every session that follows. Read broadly.
> Update your methodology. Identify one new technique to add to your toolkit."
>
> — Ghost

## Goals

Dedicated learning day: read disclosures, update methodology, identify technique gaps.
