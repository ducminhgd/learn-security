# Day 315 — CTF Web Competition Day 5: Speed and Strategy Sprint

> "Speed in CTF is not about rushing. It is about having so many patterns
> memorised that your hands move before your conscious mind has finished
> deciding. The drill builds the reflex. The reflex wins the competition."
>
> — Ghost

---

## Goals

Run a full timed competition sprint — 4 hours, as many web challenges as possible.
Focus on decision speed: how fast do you triage, prioritise, and commit to a technique?

**Time budget:** 4 hours hard limit.

---

## Sprint Rules

```
1. Spend max 5 minutes on initial recon per challenge.
2. After 5 minutes, decide: commit or skip.
3. If you commit, give it 30 minutes max.
4. After 30 minutes without progress, document and move on.
5. No external resources for the first 30 minutes on any challenge.
6. After 30 minutes on a stuck challenge: one hint or one resource allowed.
```
