# Day 354 — Report Review and Resubmit Day 4: Closing Reports and Documentation

---

## Goals

Close all resolved reports in your tracking system.
Document lessons from each resolved report.
Begin building a personal case study library from real findings.

**Time budget:** 3–4 hours.

  ___

  ___

  ___
