# Day 340 — First Programme Sprint Day 10: Sprint Close and Retrospective

---

## Goals

Close the first programme sprint cleanly.
Submit all remaining reportable findings.
Run a full retrospective to extract learnings before starting the second sprint.

**Time budget:** 4–5 hours.
