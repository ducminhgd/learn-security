# Module 12 — Post-Ghost-Level: Career Acceleration (Days 731–750)

> "You passed the Ghost Level gate. That means you can break real systems under
> real pressure. Now comes the question every serious practitioner eventually
> faces: what do you do with it?
>
> This module does not teach new attack techniques. It teaches you how to build a
> career, a reputation, and a research identity from the foundation you spent two
> years building. That work is just as important — and just as hard — as anything
> in the previous eleven modules."
>
> — Ghost

---

## Module Goal

Transition from trained practitioner to professional security researcher, red
teamer, or detection engineer. By the end of this module, you will have a
personal career plan, a public technical profile, a specialization research
roadmap, and the professional skills to operate at senior level.

## Prerequisites

- Day 730 — Ghost Level Competency Gate (passed)
- Two years of the full training programme

---

## What Success Looks Like

At the end of Day 750, you will have:

1. A written career plan with one primary specialization chosen
2. A public technical profile with at least one published write-up or advisory
3. A 30-day research plan for your chosen specialization
4. A personal methodology document
5. A security research lab that can sustain professional-grade work

That is not graduation. That is the beginning.
